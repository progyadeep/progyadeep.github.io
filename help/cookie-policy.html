Cookie Policy

Cookies are simply text data that a website can store on your web browser which can be retrieved by the website when you revisit it. Cookies are widely used by most websites today to track user activities for research & analysis, experience development and monetization.

This website SOLELY uses the cookie functionality of your web browser to store Navlogs that you create (if and only if you choose to save it) so that you can retrieve it when you come back. This data doesn't ever leave your browser. It enables you to save your Navlogs on your device for later use without having to create an account on this website or with any other third party service provider.

This website DOES NOT attempt to identify you in any way. No data is stored about your activities on this website on the server.
