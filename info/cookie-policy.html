Cookie Policy

Cookies are simply text data that a website can store on your web browser which can be retrieved by the website when you revisit it. Cookies are widely used by most websites today to track user activities for research & analysis, experience development and monetization.

This website DOES NOT store any cookie on your browser whatsoever and DOES NOT attempt to identify you in any way. No data is stored about your activities on this website on the server either.
