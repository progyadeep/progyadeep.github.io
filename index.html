About me

I'm Proggy, a multi-engine and group 1 instrument rated commercial pilot based in Canada. I'm currently trying to build hours and looking for low time pilot jobs and am open to meet up and network with all of you wonderful aviators out there! My aim is to gain as much varied experiences in aviation as I can. I am interested in becoming a mentor and instructor in the long run as I absolutely love sharing knowledge and experiences with others to help them grow and also grow with them.

I'm also a hobbyist musician playing keys, and a computer programmer.
