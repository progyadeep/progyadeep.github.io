CANADIAN HAM RADIO BASIC EXAM PREP

This page contains a collection of some reference material and the entire Amateur Radio Basic Exam Question bank (available on ISED's website) broken down in to 20 sets of 50 - for ease of going through - and an online quiz app that lets you select any of those 20 subsets of questions and quiz yourself. This doesn't offer an explanation of the topics or a study guide - it's a bare minimum effort to help you prep for the exam.

When I prepared for my HAM licence, I didn't wanna spend any money on the books. I looked up the individual topics on Wikipedia, YouTube, etc., for my own understanding and used this HAM radio prep tool - created by myself - to tackle the exam. I hope it helps you too!
